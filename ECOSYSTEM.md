# The ecosystem

Our library ([`index.json`](index.json)) is original and complete in this
repository. This file is the other half of "one source": every major skill
collection on GitHub, indexed with what it covers and how to reach it. Between
the two, one fetch finds everything.

The machine-readable version is [`ecosystem.json`](ecosystem.json): stars,
licenses, focus, and per-skill links for the official collections. Refreshed
2026-07-17.

## Why index instead of copy

Several of the largest collections, including Anthropic's and Vercel's own,
publish no license. No license means no right to copy, so a repo that pasted
them in would be built on content it cannot legally carry. Indexing is the
strongest thing that is also honest: the names, links, and counts that make
skills findable, with the content living where its authors put it.

## For agents

Fetch [`ecosystem.json`](ecosystem.json), pick a collection by focus, then
fetch its skills the same way you fetch ours. Our own entries stay the
reliable core: complete, tested, one format, no dead links.
