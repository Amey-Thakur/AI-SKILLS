Write a LinkedIn post about: {topic}

Goal: {goal}

Craft:
- First line is everything: LinkedIn shows ~2 lines before "see more", so the
  opening must earn the click. Lead with a specific hook (a result, a
  surprising claim, the start of a story), never "I am thrilled to announce"
  or "In today's world".
- Short lines and white space (LinkedIn reads better with line breaks between
  thoughts than dense paragraphs).
- Tell it through a concrete story or a specific insight, then land one clear
  takeaway the reader can use.
- End with a genuine question or invitation that fits the goal, if it earns
  its place: not forced "Agree? Comment below" bait.

Rules: professional but human, specific over generic, honest over performative.
No humble-bragging dressed as vulnerability, no corporate cliches, no
manufactured inspiration. Suggest a few relevant hashtags. Give me one strong
version plus two alternate opening lines. Ask for numbers or details if the
post would be stronger with them.
