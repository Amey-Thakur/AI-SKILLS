Outline: {topic}

Audience and takeaway: {audience}

Produce a structure that makes the writing easy:
- The one main point or goal the piece serves. Everything in the outline
  supports it; cut what does not.
- The sections in a logical order (build the argument, teach in dependency
  order, or walk the reader's journey), each with a clear purpose and the key
  points under it.
- Enough detail per section that writing is filling in, not figuring out.
- Where examples, evidence, or visuals go.

Rules: nail the structure and the single throughline before drafting (a good
outline is most of the work). Right-size to the format and audience. Flag if
the topic is too broad (suggest narrowing) or too thin. A strong opening idea
and a clear close/takeaway. For content-marketing or blog specifics see
content-outline and blog-outline.
