Write a blog post on: {topic}

Audience: {audience}
Angle / main point: {angle}

Structure:
- A title that promises a specific value, and an opening that earns the next
  paragraph (a concrete hook, a real problem, a claim worth reading on for):
  not "In today's fast-paced world".
- One clear thesis: the post makes a point, it does not survey everything.
  Every section supports it.
- Scannable body: descriptive headings, short paragraphs, concrete examples
  and specifics over abstractions. Show, then explain.
- A close that lands the takeaway and gives the reader something to do or
  think next.

Rules: write in a real voice, not generic content-mill prose. No cliches, no
filler, no padding to hit a length. Specifics and honesty over hype; if
something has a caveat, say it. Match the register to the audience (technical,
casual, executive). Aim for the length the point needs, no more. Ask for the
tone or any facts only I have if the post needs them.
