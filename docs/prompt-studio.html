Describe the job. The Studio picks the technique that fits: worked examples for sorting and extraction, step-by-step reasoning for logic, role and constraints for creative work, a strict schema for structured output: and assembles the complete prompt with the settings to run it.

Free · runs entirely in your browser · nothing you type leaves this page

Worked examples

For classification and extraction, two or three input→output pairs teach format and edge handling better than any instruction.

Step-by-step reasoning

For logic and analysis, room to think before answering measurably reduces errors; the answer stays cleanly separated.

Role & constraints

For creative work, a role plus a few hard constraints shape voice better than a paragraph of adjectives.

Output contracts

For structured output, an exact schema with a rule for unknowns prevents invented values and broken parsers.
