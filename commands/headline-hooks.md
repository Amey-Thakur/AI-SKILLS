Generate a range across proven angles:
- Benefit: the concrete outcome the reader gets.
- Curiosity gap: open a loop that only reading closes (without cheap

- Specific/number: a precise figure or specific claim (specifics out-pull

- Problem/pain: name the frustration the reader feels.
- Contrarian/surprising: challenge an assumption or reveal a counterintuitive

Rules: earn the click honestly: the headline must be one the content actually
delivers on (clickbait that does not pay off destroys trust and, over time,
reach). Specific over generic. Match the tone to the platform and audience.

underlying angle is weak, say so: no headline saves content with nothing to
promise.
